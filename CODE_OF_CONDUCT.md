# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful in feedback and discussion.
- Assuming good intent while reviewing technical work.
- Sharing context and reasoning, not just conclusions.
- Supporting newcomers and contributors from underrepresented groups.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Repeatedly derailing discussions or review threads.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and related community communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this Code of Conduct.

For serious or repeated violations, maintainers may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately through the repository maintainers.

All reports will be reviewed as promptly and fairly as possible.
